# Lilac Giveaway Bot

## Register

product

## Users

Discord server owners, moderators, community managers, and members. Administrators work mainly on desktop to schedule and supervise many simultaneous giveaways. Entrants arrive from Discord, often on mobile, to inspect eligibility, winners, and evidence that a draw was not manipulated.

## Product Purpose

Lilac runs high-volume Discord giveaways with a hard ceiling of 1,000 active or queued giveaways per server. It replaces opaque random picks with a committed participant snapshot, a future drand beacon, a deterministic selection algorithm, and a public audit trail. Success means administrators can operate quickly while any participant can independently trace how a winner was selected.

## Brand Personality

Exact, candid, and quietly playful. Operational controls should feel dependable and familiar. Public proof should feel approachable rather than cryptographic or theatrical.

## Anti-references

Avoid casino visuals, confetti-first giveaway tools, crypto dashboards, generic purple SaaS pages, glass panels, excessive card grids, hidden eligibility rules, and unverifiable claims such as "provably fair" without exposing the inputs.

## Design Principles

1. Show the evidence beside the claim.
2. Make the common moderation action obvious and reversible where possible.
3. Preserve Discord vocabulary without cloning Discord.
4. Prefer explicit status and timestamps over decorative summaries.
5. Keep public inspection available without an account.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus, reduced motion, semantic landmarks, 44-pixel mobile targets, non-color status labels, high-contrast light and dark themes, and readable identifiers that can wrap without breaking the layout.
